🐾 Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats sleep 12-16 hours a day! That’s 70% of their lives.
  • A group of cats is called a "clowder."
  • Cats have 32 muscles in each ear. This helps them rotate their ears 180 degrees!
  • Cats can make over 100 different sounds. Dogs only make about 10.
  • Cats walk on their toes. This is called "digitigrade" walking.

Popular Cat Breeds

Siamese

Known for their striking blue eyes and vocal personalities. Siamese cats are intelligent, social, and love to "talk."

Maine Coon

One of the largest domesticated cat breeds. Maine Coons are gentle giants with tufted ears and bushy tails.

Persian

Persians are known for their long, luxurious coats and sweet, gentle personalities. They require regular grooming.
